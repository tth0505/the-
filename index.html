THE BAND

One Direction - We love music

One Direction is an Anglo-Irish boy band consisting of: Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, Liam Payne, Harry Styles and Louis Tomlinson. They signed with Syco Records' Simon Cowell after forming and placing third on the seventh season of British competition The X Factor (2010). The group was later signed to Columbia Records. Driven to international success by the power of social media, the group's two albums Up All Night and Take Me Home topped the charts of most countries, with the hit singles "What Makes You Beautiful" and "Live While We're Young".
